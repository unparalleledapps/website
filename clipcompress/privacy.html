Privacy Policy

App: ClipCompress

Company: Unparalleled Apps LLC

Effective Date: August 16, 2025

We value your privacy. This policy explains what information ClipCompress processes and how.

On-Device Processing

All video compression is processed locally on your device; no videos are uploaded to our servers.

Data We Collect

We collect anonymous, aggregated analytics about how the app is used. This may include:

  • Session frequency, screen views, and general feature usage (e.g., presets vs. custom compression).
  • Anonymized, technical metadata about videos, such as resolution, duration, and file size—never the video content itself.
  • Performance metrics like compression time, success/failure rates, and error codes to improve stability and efficiency.

No personal data or video content is collected, stored, or shared.

Crash Reports & StoreKit

If you opt in to share diagnostics with developers in iOS, Apple may provide us with crash logs and limited technical data to help improve reliability. Purchases are processed by Apple's StoreKit; we receive purchase status (e.g., active subscription or lifetime entitlement) but not your full payment details.

Data Retention

Aggregated, anonymous analytics and crash data (if enabled) may be retained for up to 24 months for trend analysis, then deleted or further aggregated. We do not embed third-party analytics SDKs.

When You Contact Us

If you email us for support, we will receive the information you choose to share (such as your email address and device details) solely for troubleshooting purposes.

Sharing & Selling

We do not sell or rent your information to third parties.

Your Questions

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at contact@unparalleledapps.com.

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Effective Date” above when changes are made.
